What is a tagine and how is it traditionally cooked? 

Many Moroccan dishes take their name from a tagine, the clay or ceramic vessel in which they were traditionally cooked. They are traditionally cooked (mostly) on the stovetop but can also be placed in the oven.

04.

What is harissa and what ingredients does it usually contain? 

Originally from North Africa, harissa is a hot paste/sauce that usually contains chili peppers, red peppers, garlic, salt and olive oil.
